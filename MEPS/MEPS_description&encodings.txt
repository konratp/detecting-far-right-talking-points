The final MEP dataframe consists of the following columns:
full_name
surname
far_right (1 - far-right, 0 - non far-right) was assigned based on epg_name
years (5-year period in the EU parliament)
country_name
national_party_family (availvable only for the half of the dataset)

CAUTION: The encoding is not consistent the following columns, e.g. one party is labeled and is called
differently accross the dataset:
epg_code (European Parliament Group)
epg_name
national party_id
national_party_name
Use these collumns for manual validation only.
